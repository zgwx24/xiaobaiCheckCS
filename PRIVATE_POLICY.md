Privacy Policy for "小白看皮肤 (UU & BUFF)"

This Privacy Policy describes how "小白看皮肤 (UU & BUFF)" (the "Extension") handles your information.

Data Collection and Usage
The Extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, user data, or browsing history. All operations performed by the Extension, including reading page Document Object Model (DOM) data (such as virtual item float values, paint seeds, and sticker information) and generating inspection commands, are executed locally on your device within your browser.

Data Sharing
Because the Extension does not collect any data, no data is sold or transferred to any third parties.

Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
